Information about Project:

In my first year, I collaborated with my team on a project titled "Smart Home Automation" that utilised IoT components to develop a comprehensive solution integrating multiple applications. The project included:

  1. Smart Street Lighting: Operated using a PIR sensor for energy efficiency.
  2. Smart Irrigation System: Optimised water usage in agriculture.
  3. Smart Water Tank: Monitored and controlled water levels using an ultrasonic sensor.
  4. Smart Clothes Drying System: Used a rain sensor to protect clothes from rain.

We successfully completed the project with exceptional results, earning appreciation from our faculty, course coordinators, and KL officials and won the 3rd prize for its innovative idea and the team's hard work.
